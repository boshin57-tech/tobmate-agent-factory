# TOBMATE Gold Protocol Master Constitution

1. Verified physical reserve must precede issuance.
2. Original Investor Principal Ownership must never be silently diluted, reassigned, or destroyed.
3. Principal ownership NFT and economic-utilization FT are separate rights.
4. Every state transition must pass Business Rule Validation and Execution Authorization.
5. No liquidity operation may bypass reserve, ownership, consensus, or audit invariants.
6. Canonical ledger state must remain deterministic.
7. Every economic reward must correspond to a defined and auditable value source.
8. Treasury, insurance reserve, protocol revenue, and user assets must remain segregated.
9. Physical delivery, redemption, burn, settlement, and recovery must be traceable.
10. New modules must preserve backward-compatible constitutional invariants.
